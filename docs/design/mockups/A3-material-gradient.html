Google Material Symbols (Apache-2.0), filled variant, fetched as SVG from Iconify and inlined so each sub-shape can receive a <linearGradient> fill. Sun = warm gold (#FFD24D → #F5A623), Clouds = volumetric grey (#F3F5F8 → #C7CDD6, lighter at top). For partly-cloudy-day the sun sub-paths receive gold and the cloud sub-path receives grey — the make-or-break detail.
